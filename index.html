Welcome to Ellen Veden’s Official Website

Ellen Veden is a painter, teacher, and lifelong creative spirit. From her early years in New York’s SoHo art scene to her current life in Princeton, Ellen’s journey has always been about connecting art, culture, and community. Here you’ll find reproductions of her paintings and drawings — inspired by decades of exploration and her renewed passion for the arts.
