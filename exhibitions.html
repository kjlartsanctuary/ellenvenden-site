Exhibitions

Ellen’s exhibitions showcase her evolving body of work across different mediums, from painting and drawing to collaborative projects. Check back here for updates.

“Colors of Renewal” – Spring 2025

Location: Princeton Arts Council Gallery, Princeton, NJ

Dates: April 5 – May 30, 2025

A collection of Ellen’s recent works exploring themes of growth, change, and nature’s renewal.

“Horizons” – Summer 2025

Location: Chashama Gallery, New York, NY

Dates: July 10 – August 15, 2025

Large-scale paintings featuring abstract landscapes and architectural motifs.

“Reflections” – Fall 2024

Location: Capital Health Healing Arts Gallery, Trenton, NJ

Dates: September – December 2024

Showcasing mixed media works highlighting light, shadow, and emotional depth.

“SoHo to Princeton: A Journey in Art” – 2023

Location: Hamilton Township Library, Hamilton, NJ

A retrospective of Ellen’s journey from New York’s SoHo art scene to her current body of work.
